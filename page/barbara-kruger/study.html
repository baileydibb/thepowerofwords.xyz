Barbara Kruger

Example Work from Barbara Kruger

Kruger's artwork often consists of a black and white image, sometimes distorted, with a combination of the colours red and white captioning the image. This style is very unique to Kruger and was fairly easy to replicate.

My Work inspired by Barbara Kruger

These images were all created through Photoshop. I used the rectangle tool to create the red boxes, and for the text I mainly used Arial Bold as the font, and of course, I made sure it was white.

Bailey Dibb 2019
