Logos Study Page

Example Work from various companies

All logos are very simple, and do not have much detail. They are designed to be memorable and look nice to draw in potential customers.

My Work inspired by logos from various companies

I used Photoshop for these designs, and I tried to make them simplistic with highly contrasting colours in order to draw attention. To come up with the company names, I used a random word generator and would wait for what seemed like a feasible name for a company. Then, with the company name, I would make the logo.
